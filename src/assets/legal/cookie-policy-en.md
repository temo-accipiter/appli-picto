## 2) What is a cookie?

A cookie is a file stored on your device (computer, mobile, etc.) that allows information to be stored or retrieved. Other trackers may work similarly (localStorage, SDK, pixels, tracking tags).

## 3) Legal framework

**Non-essential cookies** (audience measurement, personalization, advertising, support) require **your prior consent** (Art. 82 "Data Protection" Act / ePrivacy Directive). You can **refuse as easily as accept** and **withdraw your consent** at any time.

## 4) Your choices

- On first visit: a banner offers **"Refuse"**, **"Configure"** and **"Accept"**.
- At any time: a "Cookie preferences" link is available in the footer and in your account.
- Proof of your choices (timestamp, version, technical identifier) is kept for 6 months.

## 6) Cookies/trackers used

### a) Strictly necessary (exempt from consent)

- **supabase-auth-token** (localStorage) — authentication session — duration: until logout — Supabase (EU hosting).
- **cookie_consent_v2** (localStorage) — consent proof — duration: 6 months — Picto App.
- **cf_clearance / \_\_cf_bm** — anti-bot protection (Cloudflare Turnstile) — duration: 30 min to 1 year — Cloudflare (EU hosting).

### b) Audience measurement (subject to consent)

- **Google Analytics 4** — audience measurement and statistics — duration: 2 years max — Google — EU hosting with potential transfers to the United States.

### c) Personalization (subject to consent)

- **user_preferences** (localStorage) — user preferences (theme, language) — duration: until deletion — Picto App.

### d) Marketing (subject to consent)

- **marketing_tracking** — advertising campaign tracking — duration: 13 months max — [Provider to be specified].

## 7) Retention period

- **Consent proof:** 6 months (in accordance with CNIL recommendations).
- **Consent choices:** 6 months, after which we will ask you again.
- **Session cookies:** until browser is closed.
- **Persistent cookies:** depending on purpose (2 years max for analytics, 13 months max for marketing).
